About Squash

Squash court: Measurements

A squash court is typically a rectangular surface (9.75mx6.4m in dimension for singles and 13.72mx7.62m for doubles) enclosed by four walls.

The wall at the back is called the back wall while the one on the front is called the front wall. The two walls on either side are side walls.
